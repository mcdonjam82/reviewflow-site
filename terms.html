2. SMS Communications & Consent

Gleam communicates with you primarily via SMS (text message). By providing your mobile phone number during signup and consenting to receive text messages, you agree to receive the following types of SMS from Gleam:

Owner Review Alerts

When a negative review (1–3 stars) is posted to your Google Business Profile, Gleam sends you a text message containing the review text and an AI-generated draft response. You reply APPROVE, EDIT [your text], or IGNORE. These alerts are sent as reviews arrive — there is no fixed frequency, but the volume depends on how many negative reviews your business receives.

Auto-Post Confirmations

When Gleam automatically posts a response to a positive review (4–5 stars), you may receive a brief confirmation text. You can disable these confirmations by contacting support.

Opting Out of SMS

You can opt out of Gleam SMS at any time by replying STOP to any message from Gleam. Once you opt out, Gleam will no longer send you review alerts or any other text messages. Note that opting out of SMS will prevent Gleam from notifying you about negative reviews requiring your approval.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Gleam does not charge you for text messages, but your mobile carrier may charge standard messaging fees. Message frequency varies based on your review volume.

TCPA Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and agreeing to receive SMS during signup, you provide your prior express consent under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) for Gleam to send you transactional text messages related to your account and review management. Your consent is not a condition of purchase — you may use Gleam without SMS by contacting support to arrange an alternative notification method. Your phone number will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

3. Staff Review Reminders

Gleam offers an optional Staff Review Reminders feature that allows you (the practice owner) to add staff members' mobile phone numbers so they receive periodic text reminders to ask customers for Google reviews.

How It Works

  • You add a staff member's name and phone number by texting Gleam (e.g., ADD Sarah 2535551234)
  • Gleam sends the staff member a consent invitation via SMS. No reminder messages are sent until the staff member replies YES.
  • Once confirmed, the staff member receives reminder messages at the cadence you set (weekly, biweekly, or monthly), sent only during business hours (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–3 PM local time)
  • Staff members can reply STOP at any time to immediately opt out of all reminders

Your Responsibility

By adding a staff member's phone number, you represent and warrant that:

  • The person is employed by or affiliated with your business
  • You have a reasonable basis for providing their phone number to Gleam for this purpose
  • You understand that Gleam will send an initial consent invitation SMS to that number, and no further messages will be sent unless the staff member opts in

Gleam processes staff phone numbers on your behalf as part of the service. You remain the data controller for your staff members' information.

Message Frequency and Opt-Out

Staff reminders are sent at most once per cadence period (weekly, biweekly, or monthly). Each message includes opt-out instructions ("Reply STOP to opt out"). Message and data rates may apply.

4. Monthly Reputation Report

On the 1st of each calendar month, Gleam sends a reputation report to the email address associated with your account. This report includes:

  • A summary of new reviews received during the previous month
  • Your average star rating
  • AI-generated analysis of common themes in customer feedback
  • A list of every review and the response posted by Gleam

Monthly reports are sent via email (not SMS) and are included at no additional cost with your subscription. You may unsubscribe from monthly reports by contacting info@lucralab.com.
