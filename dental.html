FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

Is this HIPAA compliant?+

Yes — Gleam is designed from the ground up to be HIPAA-safe for dental practices. Here's what that means in practice: Gleam never connects to your practice management software, EHR, scheduling system, or any internal patient data — ever. It only reads text that patients have already chosen to make public on Google. More importantly, the AI is explicitly instructed never to confirm, reference, or acknowledge any treatment details, diagnoses, procedures, or health information — even if a reviewer mentions specifics. If a reviewer writes "Dr. Smith fixed my root canal," the response will thank them warmly without touching the clinical content. Because Gleam only reads and responds to public reviews — never patient records or internal systems — we are not a Business Associate under HIPAA and no BAA is required.

What if the AI says something wrong or embarrassing?+

For 4 and 5-star reviews, Gleam posts responses automatically — but only after generating something professional, warm, and appropriate. The responses are deliberately crafted: no medical claims, no specific treatment references, no patient information — nothing that could create a liability or embarrass your practice. If a review contains no text (just a star rating), Gleam writes a brief, gracious acknowledgment rather than trying to be clever about nothing. The AI prompt has been carefully developed to ensure responses feel human, stay safe, and reflect well on the practice. And for negative reviews, you always approve before anything is posted — Gleam texts you the AI draft, and waits for your explicit "APPROVE" before it touches Google. You stay in control of anything sensitive.

Can I see what it posts before it goes live?+

For negative reviews (1–3 stars), yes — always, without exception. Gleam texts you the AI-generated draft, along with the original review text so you have full context. You reply with one of three commands: APPROVE to post as written, EDIT [your text] to post a custom response instead, or IGNORE to leave it unanswered. Nothing goes live until you act. For positive reviews (4–5 stars), Gleam posts automatically by default — most practices prefer this since it keeps response times fast. But if you'd rather personally review everything before it goes live, we can configure your account to require approval on all reviews. Just mention it when you sign up or contact support.

Will the responses sound robotic or templated?+

No. The AI reads the actual review and responds to what the patient said — not a template. No "Thank you for your wonderful review!" openers. The response references what the reviewer actually wrote — so every reply feels specific, not canned. The tone matches your brand voice (professional, warm, or friendly). In side-by-side comparisons, most owners find Gleam responses more thoughtful than their own rushed replies — because most teams dash off responses quickly, while Gleam takes its time every single time.

What happens if I want to cancel?+

Cancel anytime — no long-term contracts, no cancellation fees, no awkward phone calls. You can cancel directly from your billing portal with one click. Your subscription continues through the end of the current billing period, and then stops — you won't be charged again. Everything Gleam has already posted stays on your Google Business Profile permanently. Those responses are yours. We won't delete them, modify them, or remove your Google authorization retroactively. If you ever want to come back, reactivating is instant.

How long does setup take?+

Under 5 minutes — seriously. After completing payment, you'll receive an onboarding email within minutes. It contains a single setup link. Click it, and you'll be guided through two steps: sign in with the Google account connected to your Google Business Profile (this authorizes Gleam to read your reviews and post responses), then enter the mobile number where you want to receive SMS alerts for negative reviews. That's it. No software to install, no IT involvement, no dashboard to configure. Gleam starts monitoring your reviews immediately, and you'll receive a confirmation text once the connection is live. Total time from payment to active: under 5 minutes for most practices.
